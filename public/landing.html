Marine Tech Pro

Marine Tech Pro is a field diagnostic and repair assistant built for Freedom Boat Club service technicians working on Mercury and Yamaha 4-stroke outboard engines (115–300 HP).

What Marine Tech Pro does

Marine Tech Pro helps authorized Freedom Boat Club technicians diagnose and repair boat engines and onboard systems while on the water or at the dock. It is a mobile-friendly web app used during service calls to walk through structured diagnostic procedures, look up engine specifications and fault codes, and get AI-assisted guidance on repairs.

Guided Diagnostics

Step-by-step decision trees for no-start, overheat, rough-running, charging, trim/steering, and electronics issues.

Fault Code Lookup

Searchable database of Mercury and Yamaha fault codes with causes, steps, tools, and parts.

Engine Specifications

Quick reference for torque values, clearances, fluid capacities, and service intervals.

Ask a Tech (AI)

AI assistant that answers repair questions in context, grounded in the app's diagnostic knowledge base.

Who can use it

Access to Marine Tech Pro is restricted to authorized Freedom Boat Club employees, contractors, and affiliated service technicians. New accounts must be approved by an administrator before any feature can be used. This is an internal operations tool — it is not a consumer or member-facing product.

Sign in with Google

Marine Tech Pro uses Google Sign-In to authenticate approved users. The app requests only your basic Google profile (name and email address). It does not access Gmail, Drive, Calendar, or any other Google service data.
